What are electrolytes anyway?

When you begin the Keto diet and drastically cut back on carbs you eat, your body creates less insulin and your glycogen (stored carbs) is depleted. This is a good thing.

Here’s the part people don’t understand. Every gram of glycogen (stored carbs) has three grams of water are stored as well.

When you go on Keto, your body starts getting rid of the stored carbs. And when the stored carbs go, the water goes too. That’s why people lose a lot of water weight when they start Keto.

But when you flush out the water important minerals called electrolytes are excreted too.
